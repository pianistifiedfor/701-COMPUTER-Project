>What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is the act of removal of trees on a large scale to meet other needs. Deforestation has a lot of nasty side effects. Deforestation is one of the leading
causes of Global Warming, placed at a lofty 2nd rank. Mass Deforestation could end human and animal life on Earth as it not only prevents more production of oxygen and
reduction of carbon dioxide, it also adds more carbon dioxide and also destroys the habitats of many animals, which leads to death as animals are highly adapted to their
habitats and can't afford to have them destroyed. Deforestation on itself could end life on Earth without these factors as they could cause Global Warming on an alarming
scale. In any case, deforestation should certainly be reduced and new plants and trees should be planted to break the spiral of doom we are sending ourselves to.

>What are the effects of deforestation?

Effects of large-scale Deforestation:

  • Global Warming
  • Destruction of animal habitat
  • Reduced release of Oxygen
  • Abundance of Carbon Dioxide
  • Rapid heating up of the planet, leading to a thicker atmosphere with more Co2
